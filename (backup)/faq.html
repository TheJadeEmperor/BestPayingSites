Help, My Customer Did Not Receive The Download

After the customer orders from paypal, there is a small link that says "Return to Merchant", however, the link is too small and many people overlook this. And there are times that, for whatever reason, they do not receive the download email.

Our email servers may have been down during the time. We use hostmonster.com and they have a 99% uptime, however, there is still a 1% chance that the servers may be down. Another reason they do not receive the download email is probably because we are marked as spam. They need to add admin@bestpayingsites.com to their safelist.

If this happens, all you have to do is email the customer their download link: http://bestpayingsites.com/neobux/?action=download&id=transactionID

Replace transaction ID with the paypal transaction ID.

Don't panic. Situations like these happen when you do any kind of business. Most likely it is because they did not receive their ebook download. If that is the case, just send them their download link.

If they are not happy with the contents of the eBook, then that is another story. We made the ebook based on our knowledge and experiences of the PTC world, and we also gathered strategies from other ebooks we have bought. The Download Link Expired

From the time of purchase, customers have hours to download the eBook. If their link has expired and they still want to download it, the contact us, and we will add another hours to their expiration date. The script does this to prevent hackers and cheaters from downloading the eBook for free.

Invalid Download Link?

If a customer receives a refund from paypal, then their download link will no longer work. The script does this to prevent hackers and cheaters from downloading the eBook for free. When this happens, ask the customer to cancel the refund, and we will re-activate their download for them.
