Help! My Blog was marked as spam, what should I do?

If your blog is marked as spam, you can do the following to avoid blogger's spam detectors.

(1) Firstly, try to change a few of the words on the sales page. You can do this easily by editing the HTML code in your blog editor.

(2) Try making a new blogger account so that the spam detectors can't trace your new account to your old one. Before you do this, you should clear your cache, your cookies, and your internet history. Make a new gmail account and use that gmail to create a new blogger.

(3) Make a new blogger account on another browser, one that you usually do not use. For example, if you used Chrome, use Firefox instead. This way, Blogger will not be able to track your old data.
